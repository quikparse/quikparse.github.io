Our Mission

Our mission is to democratise access to big data solutions for businesses across the globe via vertical-agnostic approach and foster a data-driven growth culture.

About Us

Founded in 2016, Quikparse is a pioneer and global leader in Data-as-a-Service solutions. The integral part of our work is web data extraction using cloud computing technologies focused at helping enterprises acquire large scale structured data from the web. Currently our big data solutions are enabling some of the biggest names from a range of domains including travel, finance, health-care, marketing, analytics and more.
