Enterprise Web Crawling

Businesses of all sizes and belonging to various industries use our scraping services to grow their business.

Quikparse takes care of end-to-end extraction processes from setting up of crawlers, servers, proxies, extraction, monitoring, quality assurance, timely delivery and continuous maintenance due to structural changes in the websites. Feeding them with valuable scraped data, or providing a dedicated team of people to do the job for ensuring relevant quality with any sized data volume, is what we do to allow our clients stay ahead of the competition.

We ensure data privacy and guard it fiercely

Crawl complex websites

We crawl data from almost all kinds of websites – eCommerce, News, Job Boards, Social Networks, Forums and even ones with IP Blacklisting and Anti-Bot Measures.

High Speed Web Crawling

Our web crawling platform is built for heavy workloads. We are capable of scraping 3000 pages per second for websites with moderate anti-scraping measures. This is useful for Enterprise-grade web crawling.

Data Quality

Our automated data quality checks utilize machine learning to identify data quality issues. Over time we have invested heavily in improving our data quality processes and validation using a combination of automated and manual methods at no extra cost to customers

Intelligent Crawling

Based on machine learning based parsing algorithms, Quikparse intelligently identifies List Data, Tabular Data and Pagination Buttons without having to manually set rules, just enter the URLs.

Captcha or Language Translation? No Problems!

Yup, that’s right. We do web crawling and scraping from sites that have Captcha restrictions. We have crawled websites across 10 languages and have robust APIs for automated translation at scale.

Scalability

We do data crawling via our highly distributed web crawling platform which enables us to deliver data at desired frequency. We take care of end-to-end maintenance to overcome structural changes in the website and all you have to do is feed the data to your business
